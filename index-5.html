Exploration Suit - EVE

Concept design and game model for EVE Online
The concept for the suit is based on the planetary exploration theme.
The highpoly model was created in ZBrush alongside with Blender for hardsurface parts.
The textures and the variations for the different factions were done in Substance Painter.

Software: Zbrush, Substance Painter, Marmoset, Blender, Maya
